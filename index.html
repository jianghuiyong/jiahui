我们是做什么的？ / What do we do?

广州嘉惠鞋材有限公司地址位于中国的南大门羊城广州，广州市越秀区站西路39号新濠畔鞋材五金批发广场五楼1510室，于2001年在广州成立。在公司发展壮大的17年里，我们始终为客户提供良好的产品、良好的技术支持、健全的售后服务，致力追求环保产品，我公司主要经营PU类、布类、布料后段加工类的批发与贸易。适用于鞋、手袋、服装、室内装修及汽车内饰等用途。

Guangzhou Jiahui shoes material Co., Ltd. is located in Guangzhou, South Gate, Yangcheng, China, Yuexiu District station, Guangzhou City, No. 39, Xin hao pan shoe material hardware wholesale square, five floor, room 1510, was established in Guangzhou in 2001. In the 17 years of the company's develop-ment, we have always provided our customers with good products, good technical support, sound after-sales service, and dedicated to the pursuit of environmental protection products. Our company mainly deals with the wholesale and trade of PU, cloth, cloth and post processing.It is suitable for shoes, handbags, clothing, interior decoration and automotive interior decoration.
